Booking Process

Making a reservation is easy:

  1. Use the search bar to enter your destination and dates
  2. Browse available hotels and select your preferred option
  3. Choose your room type and number of guests
  4. Fill in your details and payment information
  5. Confirm your booking

You'll receive a confirmation email with your booking details immediately after completion.

To complete a booking, you'll need:

  • Your personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • Valid payment method (credit/debit card or PayPal)
  • Guest names (if different from the booking person)
  • Special requests or preferences (optional)

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "My Bookings"
  3. Select the booking you wish to modify
  4. Click on "Modify Booking"

Note that some changes may incur additional charges or require a new booking.

Payment & Pricing

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Debit Cards
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer (for select bookings)

All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

The charging policy depends on the hotel and rate type selected:

  • Immediate Payment: Full amount charged at time of booking
  • Deposit: Partial payment at booking, remainder at check-in
  • Pay at Hotel: No immediate charge, payment collected at check-in

The specific payment terms will be clearly displayed before you confirm your booking.

Cancellation

To cancel your booking:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "My Bookings"
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Booking"
  5. Confirm the cancellation

You'll receive an email confirmation of your cancellation.

Refund policies vary by hotel and rate type:

  • Free Cancellation: Full refund if cancelled within the specified period
  • Non-Refundable: No refund available
  • Partial Refund: Amount refunded depends on when you cancel

Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days.

Account Management

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Create a password
  4. Fill in your personal details
  5. Verify your email address

Once verified, you can start booking and managing your reservations.

To reset your password:

  1. Click "Login" and select "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for reset instructions
  4. Click the reset link and create a new password

For security reasons, reset links expire after 24 hours.

Support

You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels:

  • 24/7 Live Chat on our website
  • Email: support@tourismexplorer.com
  • Phone: 1-800-TOURISM (24/7)
  • Contact form on our website

Our average response time is under 1 hour for urgent matters.

For emergency assistance:

  1. Call our 24/7 emergency hotline: 1-800-TOURISM-911
  2. Have your booking reference number ready
  3. Explain your situation clearly
  4. Follow the instructions provided by our emergency team

We prioritize emergency cases and provide immediate assistance when needed.
